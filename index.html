Beaufort

Set Sail.

Beaufort is a third person adventure game. Players take on the role of captain of their own ship.

Command your sailors and navigate your ship across the ocean.

Choose.

You are the captain. You decide what to do, and which quests to complete.

Travel between islands to set up a trade route, take privateering contracts to help one of the factions, or plunder peaceful folk as they sail by.

Explore.

Navigate the uncharted waters of a procedurally generated, nigh-infinite ocean.

Meet a myriad of characters to trade with or fight against.

Encounter enormous monsters and arguably epic ship battles.

Work in Progress.

Beaufort is being developed by Peter Offerman. It's still in a very early stage of development, and will not be released anytime soon.
